My Journey to Coding

For me, coding, is the best combination of creative and technical. There is always new concepts to learn! The hardest part for me was finding a good mentor and guide to gettting started. At this time of writing, it has been a year since beginning Dr. Angela Yu's Udemy course Complete 2021 Web Developer Bootcamp in January 2021. Taking this course has givin me so much encouragment and confidence!

After learning the basics, I've found and from many sources say to start building projects. And it's true! That's where Frontend Mentor comes in. It's the best community for learning frontend development! I have learned so much with each challenge and with the help from the community. I'm amazed how far I've come after beginning my first challenge and it was very challenging then! All of the things I'm learning are being applied to my portfolio site.
